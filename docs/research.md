## Commercial hypothesis

Buyer hypothesis: Head of Compliance, AI Governance, Knowledge Management or RFP Operations.

Validation pricing hypothesis:

- US$1,000-3,000 for an initial sanitized diagnosis;
- US$500-1,500 per team per month for recurring evaluations.

These ranges are hypotheses. They must not appear as validated pricing until interviews and a paid pilot support them.

## Seven-day gate

- 5 target-user interviews;
- 2 sanitized workflow trials;
- 1 paid pilot or signed letter of intent;
- at least 3 users find an actionable source or policy defect;
- at least 2 request a recurring baseline or follow-up run.

Decision rule: invest only with real activation and return signal; iterate with a specific TTV or quality gap; otherwise park. Do not add features to manufacture momentum.
